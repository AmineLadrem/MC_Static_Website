The Micro Club "MC" is a student scientific club
for anyone who wants to share and develop their
creativity by living new experiences in computing.
It is by feeling this need that it was born on March 5, 1985.
The MC offers recurrent and free trainings and workshops
in the different fields of programming and development,
Animated by the students of the USTHB,As well as various
competitions which make explode their know-how in computing.
